Climate

In Vietnam, the climate in not like Japan. We only have two seasons, rain season and the dry season.

It is hot all year, so it never gets cold outside. That is why I used to go to the pool everyday!

We should always bring our sunscreen.

There will be a lot of rain on the rain season. I have seen a lot of rives on the streets.

On the dry season, it is just really hot and dry.

I think that it is really cool how Vietnam only has 2 seasons, and is hot all year.

Food

In Vietnam there are a lot of foods that are spicy. We also eat rice there. I am going to talk about some traditional foods in Vietnam.

First, we have Pho. Pho is a rice noodle and you can choose whtever topping you want on it. Popular ones are pork and beaf. It is really good and you should try it.

Next we have Banh xeo which is a crispy crepe bulging with pork, shrimp, and bean sprouts. This is my favorite one.

The last one I am going to share is Banh mi. This is basically a sandwich with some vegetables inside.It's good with some sause too.
